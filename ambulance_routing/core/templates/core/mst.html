Why MST? Both algorithms are greedy — they always pick the locally cheapest option. Both are proven to give the globally optimal (minimum weight) spanning tree. Prim's grows one connected tree; Kruskal's sorts all edges and adds them if they don't form a cycle (Union-Find).
